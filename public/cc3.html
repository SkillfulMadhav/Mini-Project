Muffler Systems

Muffler Fundamentals

The muffler is part of the exhaust system that reduces engine noise. It controls sound waves produced during combustion and improves comfort.

  • Function: Dampens exhaust sound waves.
  • Chambers & Baffles: Reflect and cancel sound frequencies.
  • Back Pressure: Must balance noise reduction with engine performance.
  • Material: Usually stainless steel for heat resistance.
