Flywheel Fundamentals

The flywheel stores rotational energy and ensures smooth engine operation by balancing power delivery.

  • Energy Storage: Stores rotational kinetic energy.
  • Stability: Reduces engine vibration.
  • Material: High-density metal for inertia.
  • Connection: Attached to crankshaft.
