Camshaft Systems

Camshaft Fundamentals

The camshaft controls the opening and closing of engine valves. It ensures air-fuel mixture enters and exhaust gases exit at the correct timing.

  • Function: Controls valve timing and duration.
  • Lobes: Eccentric shapes that push valves open.
  • Types: OHC, DOHC, OHV configurations.
  • Timing Precision: Crucial for engine efficiency and power.
